Products with optional products				 	      Date  24/07/25
______________________________________________________________________________________________
The following products are set up in Uncle Sams global product file and they have optional products.
For example, the customer orders product  “71  Six Bites”  , then we ask them to select one of the following optional products
26 – Dip Ketchup
27 – Dip Mayo
28 – Dip Chilli
29 – Dip Garlic Mayo
39 – Dip BBQ
101 – Dip 1000 Isle
110 – Dip None
The customer does not have to pay for the chosen dip.
If the product (eg “118  Kids 4 Bites”) has only one optional product, the POS system will automatically select that optional product for the customer.
