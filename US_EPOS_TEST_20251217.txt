Band 1 price of combination product  "4  Sharing Platter"  is   10.05  (B)

Definition of Combination Discount
	
	A = Amount due for all the compulsory products and the two chosen optional 
      Products for the combination product

	B = Amount due for the combination product

		Combination Discount   = A - B 			
					   =  12.70 -  10.05	
					   =  2.65
